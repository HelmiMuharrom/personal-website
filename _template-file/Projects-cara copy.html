CARA - Empowering Lives

CARA is a lifestyle and community digital platform that provides easy access for persons living with dementia and caregivers to connect to an ecosystem of solutions via a mobile application.

CARA serves as a key touchpoint for members, providing personalized support across the journey of living with and caring for dementia, empowering them to lead meaningful and purposeful lives. CARA stands for Community, Assurance, Rewards, and Acceptance.

Simply put, CARA is a mobile application/web platform that helps users with DEMENTIA connect with their caregivers and support network.

While I was working at Embreo as a Product Manager Associate Intern, the CEO assigned me to two projects:

  • CARA WEB
  • CARA Mobile App

These projects are fully corresponding to real-life applications that people use. As a product associate, my main responsibilities included:

  • Fully understanding the life cycle of products
  • Supporting and assisting project managers in achieving project goals
  • Corresponding and analyzing business/project performances by providing supporting materials for both internal and external users
