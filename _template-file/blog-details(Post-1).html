Crafting the Perfect Portfolio - My Thoughts

Having an online portfolio is essential for any creative professional. It's an opportunity to showcase your work, skills, and accomplishments, and make a great first impression with potential employers and clients. But with so many portfolio-building options out there, it can be difficult to know which one is best for you.

What You Need?

To get started, it's important to think about what kind of portfolio you want to create. Do you want to use an existing template that you can customize to your needs, or do you want to create something entirely from scratch? Consider how much time and effort you want to invest in the project and what kind of features you need to best showcase your work. Do you want a detailed and comprehensive portfolio, or a simple and streamlined one?

Once you've decided on the kind of portfolio you want to create, it's time to start building. There are many online portfolio-building tools available, from simple drag-and-drop editors to more complicated coding-based solutions. However, it's important to find the one that works best for you. Whether you choose a simple or advanced tool, make sure it's easy to use and intuitive, and that it allows you to create the kind of portfolio you have in mind.

My Tips:

When creating your portfolio, be sure to include only your best work and keep it up to date with your most recent projects. Include detailed descriptions of each piece, and be sure to highlight any relevant awards or recognition you have received. Positive feedback from clients or employers should also be included. Additionally, showcase any relevant publications or press coverage you've received, as this can further demonstrate your skills and accomplishments.

  • Decide on the kind of portfolio you want to create
  • Find an online portfolio-building tool that works best for you (I use Notion + Super.so)
  • Include only your best work and keep it up to date with your most recent projects
  • Include detailed descriptions of each piece
  • Include any relevant awards or recognition you may have received
  • Include any relevant publications or press coverage you may have received

Bottom Line 🎑

Finally, make sure your portfolio looks professional. Invest in high-quality photos and videos, and be sure to proofread all of your text. Take the time to ensure that all the elements of your portfolio come together to create a cohesive and professional presentation. Once you're done, you'll have a portfolio that demonstrates your creativity and skills, helping you stand out from the competition.
