What I've Learned

As a growth marketing intern at Kompas, my primary role is to assist the marketing team in developing and executing marketing campaigns to drive user acquisition and engagement. Some of my day-to-day activities include:

  • Conducting market research to identify potential target audiences
  • Creating and scheduling social media posts
  • Writing and editing OSM, Email Marketing, and other improvement
  • Analyzing user data to identify trends and opportunities for growth
  • Assisting with the planning and execution of day operations such as email marketing, ads copy, and more

Some Highlights

Through this internship, I have gained valuable skills in digital marketing, Email Marketing Strategies, and data-driven analysis. I have also learned the importance of collaboration and communication in a fast-paced startup environment. These skills have helped me develop a better understanding of how to develop and execute marketing strategies that are effective in driving user engagement and growth.

üèÜTeaching Assistant for Project Management

What Makes it Interesting

As a teaching assistant for the e-course bootcamp for project management, my responsibilities include helping students with course material, facilitating group discussions, and providing feedback on assignments. Some of the key skills and knowledge I am imparting to the students include:

  • Project management methodologies and best practices
  • Team collaboration and communication skills
  • Time management and prioritization techniques
  • Problem-solving and decision-making skills
  • How to handle class, which can be challenging in virtual meetings

This internship has been the best experience of my life. Through teaching and mentoring, I have gained valuable experience and a deeper understanding of project management principles. It has allowed me to effectively communicate complex concepts and ideas to others and provide constructive feedback to help others improve their skills.

PS: When you become a teaching assistant, you essentially become a participant in the course/bootcamp, but for free. And that's what I love! üòÑ It's a smart way to gain knowledge and experience without spending any money.

Lesson Learned: Juggling Two Internships

Lesson 1: Managing Time and Prioritizing Tasks

Juggling two internships simultaneously has not been without its challenges. One of the biggest challenges I faced was managing my time effectively and prioritizing tasks. To overcome this, I created a detailed schedule that allowed me to allocate time to each internship and prioritize tasks based on deadlines and importance.

Lesson 2: Maintaining Work-Life Balance

Another challenge I faced was maintaining a work-life balance. To overcome this, I made sure to take breaks and prioritize self-care activities such as exercise and spending time with friends and family.

Overall, juggling two internships has been a rewarding and valuable experience. It has provided me with valuable skills and knowledge in two different fields, as well as a deeper understanding of time management and work-life balance. I have also learned the importance of adaptability and resilience in a fast-paced and ever-changing work environment.

I have gained expertise in digital marketing, content creation, data analysis, project management, teaching, and mentoring.

Throughout this journey, I have come to appreciate the significance of effective time management, maintaining a healthy work-life balance, and cultivating adaptability and resilience. These qualities have been essential in successfully navigating the fast-paced and ever-evolving work environment.

Juggling multiple internships can be an exceptional way to gain diverse experiences and skills across different domains. By embracing these principles and remaining adaptable, I have been able to extract the maximum value from my internship experiences and develop invaluable insights and competencies that will undoubtedly shape my future career trajectory.
