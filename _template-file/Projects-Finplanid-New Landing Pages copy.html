Background

Well.. you know what you know

As the product manager for Finplan.id, I took the lead in revamping our website's landing page to increase website conversions and improve user management. This project was initiated based on valuable feedback from our customers and stakeholders, highlighting the need for an improved landing page that effectively communicates the value of our services and reduces the bounce rate.

In my role, I collaborated with cross-functional teams, including design, engineering, and content, to develop a new landing page design. I defined product requirements and created user stories to guide the development process. Additionally, I developed and managed a project roadmap to ensure the timely delivery of the new landing page. Working closely with the marketing team, we also developed and executed a comprehensive launch plan for the new landing page.

Goals

The primary goals of this project were to increase website conversions, improve user management, and enhance the overall aesthetics of the website.

Strategy 2: Leveraging Social Media and Content Creation

One of the key strategies we implemented to drive traffic and engage with our target audience was leveraging social media and content creation. We recognized the importance of digital marketing in defining our product market fit and effectively reaching our audience.

As a product manager, I spearheaded the creation of tailored content that addressed the specific needs of our users. We developed a blog and established a strong presence on social media platforms, focusing on providing educational content related to personal finance and financial planning. By consistently publishing high-quality content, we were able to attract a steady stream of new visitors to our website.

In addition to content creation, we actively utilized social media to promote our blog posts and engage with our target audience. We fostered a sense of community by responding to comments, sharing relevant content, and actively seeking feedback. This approach helped us build a loyal following on social media, resulting in a significant amount of referral traffic to our website.

For SEO

  • Conduct keyword research to understand what your target audience is searching for.
  • Optimize your website's content and structure to improve search engine rankings.
  • Build high-quality backlinks to increase your website's authority.
  • Regularly update your website with fresh and relevant content to enhance its visibility in search results.

For Social Media

  • Select the most suitable social media platforms for your target audience.
  • Create a comprehensive content strategy that aligns with your brand and resonates with your target audience.
  • Engage actively with your followers by responding to comments and messages promptly.
  • Consider utilizing paid social media advertising to reach a wider audience and boost your visibility.

Strategy 3: Measure and Optimize

As a Product Manager, one of the key takeaways is the importance of measuring your results and optimizing your strategies based on what's working and what's not. By regularly analyzing website traffic, we were able to identify the most effective channels driving traffic and the best-performing pages. This valuable data allowed us to optimize our content and marketing strategies to generate even more traffic to our website.

When it comes to measuring and optimizing, it's crucial to choose wisely. Here are some tips to consider:

  1. Set clear goals and KPIs to measure the success of your strategies.
  2. Utilize website analytics tools like Google Analytics to track user behavior on your website.
  3. Regularly analyze your data and make data-driven decisions to optimize your strategies.
  4. Continuously test and experiment with new ideas to improve your results.

Conclusion

In conclusion, achieving 1000+ new visitors at Finplan.id was not an easy task, but it was achievable by implementing the right strategies. Through defining our target audience, creating quality content, leveraging social media, and measuring and optimizing our results, we were able to attract a steady stream of new visitors to our website. As a Product Manager, it's essential to constantly evaluate and improve strategies to drive ongoing business growth.
